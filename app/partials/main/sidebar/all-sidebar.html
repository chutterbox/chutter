Chutter is a place to post interesting stuff from the web. You can easily submit links, music, or text posts to a community.
Similar to how a post belongs to a community, a community belongs to a network. A network on chutter is a group of similar communities.
Start by subscribing to networks, then refine thngs by adding/removing community subscriptions
Don't want to miss anything? Chutter sorts your massive community lists by daily user activity. So if a community is 1st or second in the list, it must be really active that day.
TL;DR - networks are like countries, communities are like states, the toolbar is your map. Use it wisely.
Chutter is still a baby, please report any bugs to /c/bugs and any network requests to /c/networkrequests!
